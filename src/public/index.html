About N-LABS.

N-LABS.

N-LABS. are facilities specifically designed to store, maintain, and manage advanced weapons inventories and military equipment. These warehouses usually have a highly secure design and are equipped with high-level security technology to protect their contents from unauthorized access. N-LABS. was designed by Ness with the primary objective of maintaining the security, availability and readiness of advanced weapons, while minimizing potential risks associated with military weapons storage.
